Author

Naruto is a comic strip (manga) character designed by Masashi Kishimoto a Japanese writer. He was born in 1974 in Okoyama and is still alive. The first chapter and book of Naruto were published by him in 1999. He’s considered as one of the greatest writers with Eichiroo Oda One piece’s creator.

Character

Naruto is quite small but very strong and brave. He has blonde bangs hair and blue eyes. He wears a headband, socks with sandals and an orange uniform. He loves Ramen and a girl from his class: the useless Sakura. His chakra type is wind and he uses kunai (sharp knives) to crush his enemies. His favorite technique is Kage Bunshin no Jutsu.

Story

Naruto is an orphan rejected by his village because his body has a fox demon sealed in it so everybody is scared of him even if he’s a great guy. But, his friendship with Sasuke will help him to be accepted as he is. The manga tells a sometimes happy or epic story but also a very sad one because a lot of people and great characters die.
